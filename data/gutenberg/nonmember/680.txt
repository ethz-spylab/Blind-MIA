_TO MY DAUGHTER FLORENCE._

[AFTER A TWELVEMONTH.]


     _"More Bed-Time Stories," Sweetest Heart,_
       _And all to you belong:_
     _All that I have and am, my dear,_
       _I give you with my song._

     _All that I have and am, my dear,_
       _Is not too much to pay_
     _As tribute to the fair, young queen_
       _Who rules my heart to
